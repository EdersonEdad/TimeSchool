June 12, 2023
Independence Day
  The Philippines will celebrate its 125th Independence Day, June 12. Official events will likely occur nationwide at government buildings and other key sites, including the Aguinaldo Shrine in Kawit, Cavite Province, the historical site of the proclamation of independence. In Metro Manila, authorities will close parts of Roxas Boulevard, as well as Katigbak Parkway, South, and Independence roads to facilitate related events and parades. Dozens to hundreds of people will probably participate in each event, especially in urban centers like Metro Manila.
