  Discipline
  A good student is disciplined. They understand that their education is a priority, and they are willing to sacrifice other activities to focus on their studies. A disciplined student knows how to set goals and stick to them. They are self-motivated and don't need external motivation from parents or teachers to succeed.
  Passionate
  A good student is passionate. They have a love for learning. They are curious and constantly asking questions. Passionate students are excited about their education and wants to learn as much as possible.
  Organization
  A good student is organised. They keep track of their assignments and due dates. The kids have a system for taking notes and keeping them organised. They also know where to find the resources they need to be successful. A well-organized student can focus on their studies and get more done less time.
  Motivation
  A good student is motivated. The kids have a reason for wanting to do well in school. They are resilient and don't give up when things get tough. They also enjoy learning and growing as a person. A motivated student is always looking for ways to improve their skills and knowledge.
  Time Management
  A good student knows how to manage their time. They can prioritise their tasks and get the most important things done first. They also know how to take breaks and not let their studies consume their entire life. A student with good time management skills can balance their schoolwork with their other responsibilities.
  Persistence
  A good student is persistent. They don't give up when things get tough. They keep trying until they find a way to succeed. A constant student is always looking for ways to improve their skills and knowledge.
